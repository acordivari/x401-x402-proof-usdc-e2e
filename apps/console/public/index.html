How authorization works

Each purchase carries a signed Intent mandate binding your verified OIDC identity to this agent wallet and a spend scope (cap, merchant, categories, expiry). The merchant verifies the signature and that Payment ⊆ Cart ⊆ Intent — and tracks cumulative spend — before settling. Out-of-scope purchases are refused, not charged.
